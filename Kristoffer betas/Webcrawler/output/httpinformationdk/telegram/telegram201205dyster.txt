De dyster om Guldpalmen i Cannes i aften
Se, hvem Thomas Vinterbergs film "Jagten" er oppe imod i hovedkonkurrencen ved filmfestivalen i Cannes.


 Det er disse film og instruktører, der konkurrerer om Guldpalmen på årets filmfestival i Cannes, som søndag kulminerer med prisuddelingen:
* "Amour" (Love) - Michael Haneke
* "Baad El Mawekaa" (After The Battle) - Yousry Nasrallah
* "Cosmopolis" - David Cronenberg
* "Da-Reun Na-Ra-E-Suh" (In Another Country) - Hong Sangsoo
* "De Rouille Et D'os" (Rust and Bone) - Jacques Audiard
* "Do-Nui Mat" (The Taste Of Money) - Im Sang-Soo
* "Dupã Dealuri" (Beyond The Hills) - Cristian Mungiu
* "Holy Motors" - Leos Carax
* "Jagten" - Thomas Vinterberg
* "Killing Them Softly" - Andrew Dominik
* "Lawless" - John Hillcoat
* "Like Someone In Love" - Abbas Kiarostami
* "Moonrise Kingdom" - Wes Anderson
* "Mud" - Jeff Nichols
* "On The Road" - Walter Salles
* "Paradies: Liebe" (Paradise: Love) - Ulrich Seidl
* "Post Tenebras Lux" - Carlos Reygadas
* "Reality" - Matteo Garrone
* "The Angels' Share" - Ken Loach
* "The Paperboy" - Lee Daniels
* "V Tumane" (In The Fog) - Sergei Loznitsa
* "Vous N'avez Encore Rien Vu" (You Ain't Seen Nothin' Yet!) - Alain Resnais
